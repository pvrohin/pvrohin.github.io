<div style="text-align: center;"><h1>PSG Self-Driving Car</h1></div>

The **PSG Self-Driving Car** is an autonomous vehicle developed at **PSG College of Technology** capable of navigating the campus environment. The system integrates a perception stack covering lane detection, binary segmentation, signboard recognition, and human detection, deployed on a custom hardware platform with multiple sensors.
